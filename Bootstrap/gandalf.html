THE WIZARD

WORLD MOST POWERFUL MAGIC SPELLS

Gandalf is the most powerful wizard in Middle Earth in the book and this book would be fitting for him because he is cautious about using his power for unnecessary reasons. Gandalf is knowledgable and knwos that he needs to keep his powers in check for the betterment of everybody.

SPREADING WISDOM

Gandalf acts like a father figure throughout the story, guiding Bilbo and the Dwarves whenever they seem to lose their path. Gandalf actually tries to impart some of his wisdom into Bilbo because he sees an adventurer inside him, waiting to burst out of its shell.

BEING SLY

Gandalf has shown throughout the story that he is clever and cunning in every approach he has taken to solve a problem. One example of this is when A great example of this is when Thorin's company prepares to go to the Carrock to meet Beorn, who does not like strangers very much. Instead of all fifteen of them showing up on Beorn's doorstep at once, Gandalf hatches a plan for the dwarves to arrive in pairs in five minute intervals as Gandalf tells Beorn the exciting tale of their adventure so far.

HOW DO I GET SOMEONE TO GO ON A TRIP?

Gandalf knew that if he were to just ask Bilbo if he wanted to go on the trip, Bilbo would have immediately said no. SO when Gandalf brought all of the Dwarves into Bilbo's house unannounced, Bilbo was forced to be kind to the visitors and the Dwarves assumed that he would go with them, which made Bilbo have no choice but to go with them.
