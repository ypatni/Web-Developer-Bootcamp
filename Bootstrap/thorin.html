10 EPIC WAYS TO KILL A DRAGON

Thorin was obsessed with killing Smaug because Smaug lay in the place where his grandfather kept his riches. Throin wanted to be the one to kill Smaug because he felt that it would make him more honorable. Throughout the entire journey, one can see that Thorin only thought about the dragon he needed to face.

HOW MUCH IS THE ARKENSTONE WORTH

Thorin believes that the Arkenstone is worth an amazing fortune of money and immediately tries to find it when he goes into the castle. When he realizes that Bilbo keeps it to trade with Bard, he becomes furious, showing his greed during the war.

HOW MUCH GOLD DOES SMAUG HAVE?

Thorin's greed is seen when he says the lines "To the treasure of my people no man has a claim, because Smaug who stole it from us also robbed him of life or home. The treasure was not his that his evil deeds should be amended with a share of it." He refuses to pay the people who have helped him with the gold he had just acquired and is no longer the benevolent leader the reader once thought he was.

THE MAP OF EREBOR

Thorin was unable to understand the map of Erebor and even the Elves, the most skilled map creators, were not able to decipher the ancient symbols. Thorin could have used this website to fin out more about the map of Erebor.
